About Me

My expertise lies in mathematical modeling, computational geometry, and machine learning in the areas of engineering and applied physics. I currently lead research activities at Axya in Montréal. Before that I was a graduate student of Yaoyao Fiona Zhao at McGill University and Clément Fortin at Skoltech.

For the parametric modeling software for cellular structures, see LatticeQuery.
